Crystal Collector Game!!

At the begining of each game you will be given a random number.

There are 4 crystals, each worth 5 points.

When you click on a crystal, it's number value will be apointed to you.

The goal is to end up with the same number of points as the computer.

If you go over, it's Game Over.

Start by clicking on one of the crystals to add points to your scorecard, Have fun!
